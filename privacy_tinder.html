Privacy Policy

Auto Liker for Tinder & Boo – Chrome Extension

What data do we collect?

The Auto Liker for Tinder & Boo extension does not collect or share personal user data. The only information stored is the number of "Likes" (likeCount) and the activation status (isActive), which are saved locally in your browser using the Chrome Storage API (chrome.storage.local).

How is the data used?

The data (number of Likes and activation status) is used solely to display the progress and status of the Auto Like feature on the Tinder and Boo platforms. This data is not sent or shared with third parties or any servers.

Access and deletion

All settings and data are stored exclusively in the user's browser and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing Chrome's data.
